How to Calculate Your SRM GPA

Follow these simple steps to calculate your GPA:

  1. Enter your grade for each course (e.g., A+, A, B+, etc.).
  2. Enter the credits for each course (usually between 1 and 10).
  3. Click "Add Another Course" if you have more courses.
  4. Click "Calculate GPA" to see your result.

About SRM University Grading System

SRM University uses a 10-point grading system. Here's how grades are converted to grade points:

  • A+ = 10
  • A = 9
  • B+ = 8
  • B = 7
  • C = 6
  • F = 0 (Fail)
